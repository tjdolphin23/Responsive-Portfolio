About Me

Tom Dolphin was born and raised in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. After school he pursed a career in golf industry as an assistant golf professional. After leaving golf he bounced around working in insurance, finance, and manufacturing. Living and working in Iowa for so many years, Tom, his wife, son, and dog named Duke pack their bags for Phoenix. Working odd jobs and being a part-time stay at home father, Tom began University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp. This began an exciting adventure into the world of development.
