About Me

Tom Dolphin was born and raised in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. After college Tom pursed a career in the golf industry as an assistant golf professional at Elmcrest Country Club. After his time the golf business, Tom worked in financial services industry at Transamerica in the New Business Department of Life Insurance and at ING (now Voya) at Marketing/Sales Department. Then, he worked at Schneider Electric as internal customer support. In February 2016 his family moved to Arizona where he’s spent much of his time as stay-at-home father and working side jobs.


A year after moving from Iowa to Phoenix, Tom began dabbling in coding. Spending time going through Code School learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Eventually, he became serious about web development as a career and enrolled in Coding Bootcamp through the University of Arizona. During the U of A coding program, he learned a variety of languages; HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, JQuery, API’s, Express, MySQL, MongoDB, Sequelize, React, Handlebars, Java, and basics of computer science.
